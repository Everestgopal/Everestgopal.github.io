01 — About Me

Spatial Analyst,
Physicist &
GIS Professional


I'm Gopal Bhusal, a GIS & Remote Sensing enthusiast with a deep passion for environmental research and spatial science. I hold an MS in Geographic Information Science from Saint Louis University and an MS in Physics from Tribhuvan University — a combination that lets me approach geospatial problems with both technical rigor and scientific curiosity.

I love working with satellite imagery, LiDAR point clouds, and spatial datasets to create maps and reports that tell a story. From processing raw LiDAR data to build precise 3D point clouds, running QA/QC workflows, and performing spatial analysis in ArcGIS Pro, to developing geodatabases that organize and visualize field-collected data — I enjoy every step of turning raw data into actionable insights.

My background spans environmental modeling, atmospheric science, hydrological research, and remote sensing — always driven by the goal of using geospatial tools to better understand and protect our changing planet.
