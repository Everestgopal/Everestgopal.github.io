I'm Gopal Bhusal, a GIS analyst and environmental researcher at Saint Louis University. My work sits at the crossroads of remote sensing, environmental modeling, and spatial storytelling.

From quantifying urban sprawl in the Kathmandu Valley to predicting air pollution with deep learning, I apply advanced spatial methods to pressing environmental challenges. I believe good maps — built on rigorous data — drive meaningful policy and change.
